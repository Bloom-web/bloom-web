What does it mean to reimagine and innovate the data viewing experience through an app on a device?  This was a crucial question tackled by Heather Zindel, CEO of Bloom Consulting Group, at the 2015 Drive/ conference.  She shared the stage with Grad Conn, the CMO Lead for the Microsoft U.S. Central Marketing Organization, to share insights on why simplifying the way we visualize information is at the heart of future innovation.

The Drive/ event is held annually and attracts over 750 business professionals to deepen their knowledge of data, analytics, modeling, visualization, and so on.  Attendees gain access to the most up-to-date content in the business as well as the opportunity to interact and learn from industry leaders.
