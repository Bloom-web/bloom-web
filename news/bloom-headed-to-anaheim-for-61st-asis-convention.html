Bloom Headed to Anaheim for the 61st Annual ASIS International Convention Sept. 28-Oct.1

September 18, 2015  |  by Rachelle Raymond

Bloom Consulting Group is wheels-up and in route to Anaheim, California to attend the ASIS International convention.  Bloom will be located in booth #1650 in the ASIS expo hall and will be presenting a live demo of the new AS Solution app as well as other samples of solutions for the modern workplace and how they can be integrated in to the security industry. 

“Our company is growing at a rapid pace and security is one of the verticals Bloom is focused on,” said Heather Zindel, CEO and Principal of Bloom.  “We are looking forward to showing ASIS attendees how useful our solutions are for visualizing data, streamlining applications across devices, and supporting the modern workplace.”  

In the modern workplace, it’s all about mobility and experience which is exactly what the Apportals Bloom is developing are focused on.  The security industry can make substantial use of this technology as it can be difficult to paint a picture for potential clients when it comes to the story behind a security organization.   Come visit Bloom Consulting Group at booth #1650 for your own hands-on experience.
