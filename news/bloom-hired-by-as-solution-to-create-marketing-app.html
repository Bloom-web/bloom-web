Bloom Hired by AS Solution to Create Marketing App

September 22, 2015  |  by Rachelle Raymond

AS Solution is an industry leader in global executive protection and security, with a wide range of solutions including the recent addition of security applications that take their services mobile.  AS Solution needed something developed in time for the ASIS International conference they attend annually to draw in new clients. Bloom Consulting Group was enlisted to create a sharp marketing application that will be used for years to come by AS Solution. 

The objective was to create an experience that could be used to tell the story of AS Solution and facilitate a deeper conversation between potential clients and AS executives.  “Both the solutions and the team at AS are very unique so we really wanted to create an app that matched that personality,” said Rachelle Raymond, Project Manager at Bloom.  “The design and development were very collaborative and the end result perfectly matched what the client was hoping for.”

The AS Solution app will be utilized as a sales and marketing tool for both current and potential clients.  The newest marketing materials and data points are available at the touch of a finger and can be updated in real-time as it is an Azure driven application.  “For years, we have struggled with the ability to show customers that we go far beyond executive protection,” said Christian West, Founder and CEO of AS Solution.  “With this new app, we are able to create a beautiful interactive story-telling experience that shows the full span of solutions and technology we offer and we couldn’t be more excited about it.”
