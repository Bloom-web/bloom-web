Bloom Appoints Bob Davisson as a Project Manager

July 6, 2017

Bloom Consulting Group is excited to announce the hiring of Bob Davisson as a Project Manager for the firm. Of the 13 years of consulting experience with Microsoft he has spent 10+ of those consulting in a PMO capacity with the U.S. OEM team.

Heather Zindel, CEO for Bloom Consulting Group says, "Bob's positive attitude, knowledge of the various partner programs and understanding of incentive programs is a nice complement to our business capabilities."

Prior to joining Bloom, Bob worked most recently as a project manager with the Services Business Desk to build out an audit tool to check for sales policy violations in the field.
