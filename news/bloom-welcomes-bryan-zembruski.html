Bloom Appoints Bryan Zembruski as a Consultant

January 11, 2016

Bloom is pleased to announce that it has hired Bryan Zembruski as a Consultant with the firm. His 10+ years of project management experience has enabled him to build exceptional organization and project management skills.

Heather Zindel, CEO for Bloom Consulting Group says, "Brian's experience with Data Protection and Global Project Management makes him a perfect fit for our team here at Bloom."

Prior to joining Bloom, Bryan served as a Consultant Project Manager in IPG at Microsoft. He also spent time managing multiple international projects relating to customer acquisition and retention, partner community building, as well as policy creation and implementation.
