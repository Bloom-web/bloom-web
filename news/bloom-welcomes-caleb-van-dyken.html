Bloom Appoints Caleb Van Dyken as Scrum Master and Product Owner

August 10, 2015  |  by Rachelle Raymond

Bloom welcomes Caleb Van Dyken, a deeply experienced product manager and former Combat Pilot in the US Army, as a Scrum Master and Product Owner. “Caleb truly has his pulse on emerging technologies in consumer electronics and entertainment and we are looking forward to him optimizing our client’s business processes,” said Heather Zindel, CEO of Bloom Consulting Group.

Caleb is a Certified Project Management Professional (PMP), Certified Scrum Master (CSM), and a Certified Scrum Product Owner (CSPO).  His former work included project management and consulting for large organizations such as Technicolor Digital Delivery Services and Verizon Digital Media Services.
