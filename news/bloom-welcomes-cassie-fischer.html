Bloom Welcomes Cassie Fischer as a Project Manager at Redmond Headquarters

June 17, 2015  |  by Sukhie Patel

Bloom is pleased to announce Cassie Fischer has been hired as a Project Manager.  Cassie brings over five years of experience in IT les and marketing.  “We pride ourselves on the diverse background of knowledge and expertise our project managers come equipped with and Cassie is no exception to this,” said Sara Bush, PM Group Director at Bloom Consulting Group.

Cassie’s earlier work focused on increasing product awareness through promotions and growth strategies as an account specialist in IT sales and marketing.  Cassie completed her undergraduate studies at Washington State University and is currently working on her Master’s in Business Administration (MBA) at Murray State University.
