Bloom Hires Experience Architect Hoby Van Hoose

September 01, 2015  |  by Rachelle Raymond

Bloom is pleased to announce Hoby Van Hoose has been hired as an Experience Architect.  Hoby has over 20 years of experience in design and takes pride in creating a unique, delightful experience for users. “Hoby has an amazing eye for design and puts an immense amount of thought in to the work he creates,” said Heather Zindel, CEO of Bloom Consulting Group.  “He is also extremely collaborative with our clients and provides them with unique, strategic design ideas that remain in line with their business objectives which is very valuable.”

Hoby will be responsible for translating the ideas and business objectives of Bloom clients into visually pleasing and user friendly virtual experiences.
