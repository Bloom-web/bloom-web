Bloom Hires Windows Application and User Experience Developer Mason Tejera

August 10, 2015  |  by Rachelle Raymond

Bloom is excited to announce a new User Experience Developer, Mason Tejera, as the newest addition to the company. “Mason has everything you look for in a UX developer—sharp, innovative, and creative—providing a truly unique user experience to our Apportals,” said Andrew Herb, Vice President of Application Development and Business Intelligence at Bloom Consulting Group.

Mason has been building mobile Windows Apps since 2012. Notably, he has built dashboards for high level executives, on-the-go sales tools, and precision data capture apps. As a UX Developer at Bloom, he will be responsible for driving creative data visualization in support of the extended Consultant and Business Intelligence teams.
