Bloom Welcomes Rachelle Raymond as a Project Manager

August 10, 2015  |  by Cassie Fischer

Bloom is pleased to announce a new project manager, Rachelle Raymond.  Rachelle has a unique background, coming from a company that specializes in complex program and corporate event management as well as custom website development.  She has a depth of experience with large organizations including Microsoft, Concur, AT&T, and Polycom.

“We noticed early-on that Rachelle has a great eye for visualization and we are looking forward to her bringing those skills to our clients to help them recognize and achieve their goals,” said Heather Zindel, CEO of Bloom Consulting Group.  “Rachelle will also be training to become a certified Scrum Product Owner to take advantage of her creativity and knack for understanding a client’s business objectives.”  She joins the growing ranks of well-respected Project Managers Bloom is known for.
