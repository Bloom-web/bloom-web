Bloom Appoints Sara Bush as Consulting and PM Group Director

June 15, 2015  |  by Sukhie Patel

Bloom is pleased to announce Sara Bush has accepted the role of Consulting and PM Group Director.  Sara is a Certified Project Management Professional (PMP), a Certified Scrum Product Owner (CSPO) and a Master’s in Business Administration (MBA). Sara joined the firm back in 2012 as a Senior Project Manager and Consultant and has over two decades of experience in the technology industry. 

“Sara is a sharp, innovative leader who is equally dedicated to her work and our team of project managers and consultants,” said Heather Zindel, CEO of Bloom Consulting Group.  “I felt extremely confident in appointing her in this role and look forward to partnering with her to shape our organization as it grows.” 

When asked why she wanted to take on this role, Sara noted, “The Group Director role was of particular interest to me because I believe in our company, our people and the services we provide.  This role provides the platform to develop continued, deep support for our employees and clients, enabling me to mentor and serve the great people who work here while engaging more broadly with those who utilize our services.  We are the gold standard in Consulting and Project Management and this role is an exciting opportunity to provide thought leadership in operations and client engagement.” 

Sara brings over 20 years of experience in the technology industry with an extensive background in product development, marketing, and operations management.  She is the true definition of someone who leads by example as she maintains the utmost professionalism, positive attitude, and commitment to her work.  “Sara is a wonderful mentor and I appreciate her guidance, especially while I am pursuing my own MBA outside of work,” said Cassie Fisher, Project Manager at Bloom.
