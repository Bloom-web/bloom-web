Bloom Appoints Damon Edmondson as a Project Manager

October 19, 2016

Bloom is pleased to announce that it has hired Damon Edmondson as a Project Manager with the firm. He has worked as a project manager for more than 10 years with more than 5 of those consulting with Microsoft.

Heather Zindel, CEO for Bloom Consulting Group says, "Damon brings a solid set of project management and analyst skills to Bloom."

Prior to joining Bloom, Damon spent a good deal of time as a consulting project manager at Microsoft.
