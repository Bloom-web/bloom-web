Bloom Appoints Marcos Raya as an Associate Consultant

September 6, 2017

Bloom is pleased to announce the hiring of Marcos Raya as an Associate Consultant with the firm. Of the 15 years of consulting experience he has spent 14 of those consulting in various capacities at Microsoft with the Worldwide EPG team.

Heather Zindel, CEO for Bloom Consulting Group says, "Marcos' breadth of knowledge in the partner realm and his marketing experience pairs very well with our focus on Strategic Consulting here at Bloom."

Prior to joining Bloom, Marcos worked as the VP of Strategic Accounts, managing the Microsoft relationship and business development for Q Analysts. Before that role he consulted at Microsoft as the Worldwide EPG Alliance Partner Manager building Alliance Partner relationships with major Worldwide OEM partners, and Go-To-Market programs with Azure, Office, and the Windows BG's.
